The Journey

A six-week arc guiding you from self-judgment to reverence, from surviving to rooted, joyful embodiment.

  1. The Portal & The Pause

    Begin in stillness. Ground, breathe, be held. Remember who you are beneath all the change.

  2. Resistance Is Sacred

    Honor resistance as teacher. Support your nervous system and learn to feel safe in the unknown.

  3. The Body Remembers

    Process your birth story somatically. Release what’s still held. Move with deep compassion.

  4. Reclaiming the Sacred Body

    Speak love to your skin. Rewrite the narrative. Dance as an act of reverence and return.

  5. Dancing the Elements

    Move with earth, air, fire, water, and spirit. Root, rise, and remember your joy.

  6. The Sacred Return

    A ceremony just for you. Integrate, celebrate, and come home to your transformed body.
